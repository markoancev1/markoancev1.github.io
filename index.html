The details: When does the Steam summer sale 2019 start and end?
This year’s sale – which is likely to see huge discounts on blockbuster games and smaller, indie titles alike – is due to kick off on 25 June.
According to Steam Database, the sale will begin at 10am PST (6pm BST) on that date, and run for two weeks, which takes us to 9 July.
It’s likely the leak will turn out to be correct; Valve (the development company behind Steam) shares the dates of its sales well ahead of schedule to game developers to avoid any crossover with Steam’s sales and the developers’ own.
They ask that developers don’t share the details, but obviously this is very hard to police.

  • Every day there will be a free game
  • If you buy a game you can get a -5% discount.
